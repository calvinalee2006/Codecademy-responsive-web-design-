At mechanic build we are all about building your strength and endurance to get through the working world. We strive to make our clients stronger with our open gym, cardio vascular area, and we also have personal trainers to help you build and become the healthier person you would like to be. We also offer nutritional snacks and supplements to help with exhaustion and give your muscles the needed nutrition to make it the day through an keep your gains.
Our owner Calvin Lee has been actively working as a mechanic for the last 15 years. Has also been exercising just as long, and wanted to show others his knowledge and information about his journey to fitness.
